System Architecture

The system architecture comprises a quadruped robot and a UR5e robotic arm. The quadruped is outfitted with a sensor suite, including a camera and an ultrasonic sensor, to enable sensor fusion. The UR5e arm is responsible for repositioning the quadruped back to its original location to repeat the process.
